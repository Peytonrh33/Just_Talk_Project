Just Talking

About Us

This page is for veterans or for anyone who just need to sit down with random people and let what's bothering them out. Life gets rough sometimes and we need somewhere to go that's not popular and where there might just only be people who genuinely are seeking to talk with someone. You can continue as a guest to see the topics that are on the page but you will not be able to access the topics in order to protect the privacy of our registered users.

Ground Rules

  • No toxicity
  • No Discrimination of any kind
  • Please keep cursing at a minimum
  • Golden Rule
  • This is a safe space your privacy is our top priority
  • Most importantly everyone will be respectful
